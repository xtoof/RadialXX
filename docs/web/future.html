We hope to move Radial++ to a high performance computing, for this reason will be necessesary to do research or incorporate at least the following topics: preconditioning, domain decomposition, gmres, fast multipoles, asynchronous domain decomposition, Rippa method, compact support RBFs, efficient data structures like octree or kd-tree, time adaptive and node adaptive.

We stress that the above topics conform the central part of research in the field of radial basis functions. In addition, various of these previous topics are begin undertaken in the project Methods of Radial Basis Functions for the Numerical Solution of Partial Differential Equations pderbf.

Radial++ born free and will continue in development for many many years.
